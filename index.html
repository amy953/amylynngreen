My story

I've been the therapist in the room — and the patient who needed one.

I'm a licensed clinical social worker and a certified perinatal mental health specialist. I'm also the founder of a women's mental health company, a mother, and someone who has needed care as much as I've given it.

I started writing because I kept hearing the same words: "It's just hormones." "It's just stress." "It's just a phase." When the truth was always bigger — and always about context. The same symptoms mean completely different things in different seasons of a woman's life.

This is where I write about those seasons: honestly, clinically, and from the inside.

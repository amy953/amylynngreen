Speaking & Press

On stage, in print, and on the mic.

I'm Amy Green — LCSW, PMH-C, and founder of Mamaya Health. I speak on women's mental health across the lifespan, maternal mental health, and the realities of building and funding a mission-driven company. I've built and scaled a mental health company recognized on the Inc. 500 and through EY's Entrepreneurial Winning Women program — and I bring that same candor to every stage and every conversation.

☾ Women's brain health across the lifespan
☾ The mind-body connection — chronic illness, PCOS, endometriosis & mental health
☾ Supporting women in the workplace — and balancing career with family
☾ Building integrated, whole-person care models
☾ Culturally responsive, equitable access to care
☾ Trauma-informed care & expanding access to mental health
☾ Scaling clinical teams while staying human
